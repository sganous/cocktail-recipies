Whiskey Sour

A beautifully silky and refreshing whiskey cocktail.

This is the most iconic sour cocktails that lends itself to many riffs and modifications
With just a few simple ingredients, you have a delicous whiskey cocktail that tames the stern
bite of a whiskey, but still features the spirit, making this a great drink for those hesitant
to dive into whiskey.

Recipe

  • 2 ounces of bourbon
  • 3/4 ounce lemon juice
  • 3/4 ounce simple syrup
  • 1 small egg white
  • Angostura Bitters

Steps

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a cocktail shaker and dry shake (without ice) for at least 15 seconds.
  2. Add ice to the shaker and shake well.
  3. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
  4. Gently add 3-4 drops of Angostura Bitters on top of the egg foam.
